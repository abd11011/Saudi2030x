About Saudi2030X

Saudi2030X is a next-generation intelligent platform connecting smart logistics, heavy equipment, solar energy systems, and AI-powered gold trading — all aligned with Saudi Vision 2030.

Saudi2030X acts as a smart infrastructure layer to secure and digitize real-world operations across logistics, construction, energy, and trading. From smart Proof of Delivery and anti-theft systems to AI gold hedging engines, the platform is engineered for the next economy.

Core Sectors

One unified platform, four powerful verticals.

Smart Logistics & Transport Intelligent matching between shippers and carriers, real-time tracking, ETA predictions, digital Proof of Delivery, and automated trip risk scoring.
Heavy Equipment & Contracting Digital layer for renting and monitoring heavy equipment usage and abuse, with verifiable usage logs and performance analytics.
Solar Energy & Infrastructure Digital phase verification, performance monitoring, and contract-proofing for solar projects from design to O&M.
AI Gold Trading & Hedging AI-powered strategies for trading and hedging gold, combining advanced candlestick logic, volatility filters, and risk management models.

Intelligent Technology Stack

Saudi2030X is not just a platform — it is a layered intelligence system built on AI, blockchain, and IoT.

Smart e-POD (Proof of Delivery) Digital signatures, GPS validation, multi-party approval, and immutable records anchored on-chain.
Driver & Truck Legitimacy Engine Automated checks on driver identity, license status, and truck compliance before loading and dispatch.
Anti-Theft & Fuel Integrity System AI models detect anomalies in routes, fuel consumption, and time patterns to flag potential fuel or cargo theft.
Real-time ETA & Delay Prediction Predictive models estimate arrival times to loading and offloading points with continuous recalibration.
AI Trading & Hedging Engine for Gold Algorithms built around candle logic, volatility, and hedging rules to support smart gold strategies.
Cross-Sector Compliance Layer Unified auditability and compliance across logistics, equipment, energy, and trading.

عن Saudi2030X

Saudi2030X منصة ذكية من الجيل القادم تربط بين اللوجستيات الذكية، تأجير وتشغيل المعدات الثقيلة، أنظمة الطاقة الشمسية، وتداول الذهب المدعوم بالذكاء الاصطناعي، ضمن إطار رؤية المملكة 2030.

تعمل Saudi2030X كطبقة بنية تحتية رقمية آمنة لتنظيم وتوثيق العمليات الواقعية في مجالات النقل، المقاولات، الطاقة والتداول. من إثبات التسليم الإلكتروني (e-POD) وأنظمة منع سرقة الشحنات والمحروقات، إلى محركات التحوّط والتداول الذكي في الذهب، تم تصميم المنصة لخدمة اقتصاد المستقبل.
